Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy’s entry on ‘Information’ has provided a broader perspective beyond the technical definition in Shannon’s theory. I’ve learned about different philosophical approaches to understanding information, including its nature, how it’s represented, and its role in various contexts. This will be valuable for a more holistic view of information within the meta-framework.
